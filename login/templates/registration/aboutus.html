The Team

Richelle Penensoro, Chrizvique Villarin, Justin Yong (CEO, founder)

Richelle Penensoro, Chrizvique Villarin, and Justin Yong are the CEO's and founder of Instagram Clone,
a community of more than 800 million who capture and share the world's moments on the service. They focus
on building products that bring out the creativity in all of us and are responsible for the company's overall
vision and strategy as well as day-to-day operations.

Since the beginning, Richelle, Chrizvique, and Justin have focused on simplicity and inspiring creativity through
solving problems with thoughtful product design. As a result, Instagram Clone has become the home for visual
storytelling for everyone from celebrities, newsrooms and brands, to teens, musicians and anyone with a creative
passion.

Prior to founding Instagram Clone, Richelle, Chrizvique, and Justin worked at Meebo as user experience designers
and front-end engineers, were part of the startup Odeo which later became Twitter, and spent two years at Google
working on products like Gmail and Google Reader. They graduated from Stanford University with a Bachelor of
Science in Management Science & Engineering and serve on the boards of Walmart and KCRW.
